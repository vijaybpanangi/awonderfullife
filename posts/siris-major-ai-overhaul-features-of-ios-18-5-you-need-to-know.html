Siri's Major AI Overhaul: Features of iOS 18.5 You Need to Know

VIJAY PANANGIPALLY · MARCH 03, 2025 · 5 MIN READ

Apple is making big moves in artificial intelligence, and if the latest reports are true, Siri is about to get a serious upgrade. With the upcoming release of iOS 18.5, Apple is expected to introduce a new AI-powered system called Apple Intelligence, designed to make Siri smarter, more intuitive, and more useful than ever before.

But what does this mean for iPhone users? How will this new AI update change the way we use Siri in our daily lives? Let’s break it down in simple terms.

Apple Intelligence: What’s Coming to Siri?

Siri has been around for over a decade, but let’s be honest: compared to AI assistants like ChatGPT and Google Assistant, it’s often felt outdated. That’s about to change with Apple Intelligence, Apple’s ambitious new AI system set to roll out in iOS 18.5.

Rumours suggest this upgrade will bring:

  • Smarter responses: Siri will be better at understanding context and following multi-step commands.
  • More natural conversations: Expect Siri to sound more human, with improved speech patterns and tone.
  • Tighter integration with Apple apps: Siri will work more seamlessly across Messages, Mail, Calendar, Safari, and other built-in apps.
  • Better personalization: Siri will learn more about your habits and preferences, helping you faster and more efficiently.

This is more than a minor update. It’s a complete overhaul designed to make Siri actually useful in everyday life.

Why Is Apple Upgrading Siri Now?

Apple has been playing catch-up in the AI race. While companies like OpenAI, Google, and Microsoft have been pushing AI-powered tools into their products, Apple has taken a more cautious approach.

This could be because Apple prioritizes privacy and security, meaning they don’t rush to adopt every new technology until they’re sure it meets their strict standards. But with customer demand for smarter AI assistants growing, they’re finally bringing major AI improvements to Siri.

With the launch of Apple Intelligence in iOS 18.5, Apple is signalling that they’re ready to compete with the best AI assistants on the market.

How Will Siri’s AI Upgrade Benefit You?

Now, let’s talk about how this upgrade will actually make your life easier.

1. Smarter Multitasking

Have you ever asked Siri to do something, only for it to completely misunderstand or give you a useless answer? Those days might be coming to an end. Siri’s improved contextual awareness should allow for:

  • Following complex commands: You could say, “Remind me to buy groceries when I get off work and also text Emily about dinner,” and Siri will handle both tasks correctly.
  • Better app integration: Want to schedule a meeting, send an email, and look up flight times without switching between apps? Siri may soon juggle multiple tasks without breaking a sweat.

2. More Natural Conversations

Right now, using Siri can feel robotic. You ask a question, get a basic response, and that’s it. But with Apple Intelligence, Siri may start feeling more like an actual assistant.

  • More natural responses: Instead of stiff, one-line answers, Siri’s responses may become more fluid and conversational.
  • Follow-up questions: If you ask for nearby restaurants, Siri might ask what cuisine you’re in the mood for instead of listing random options.

This could make Siri much easier (and more enjoyable!) to interact with.

3. Smarter Suggestions Based on Your Habits

Apple already uses AI to improve recommendations in apps like Apple Music and Photos, but Siri might take this further. Imagine getting personalized reminders based on your routine:

  • “It looks like you usually leave for work around 8 AM. Would you like me to set a reminder for traffic updates?”
  • “You have a meeting at 2 PM. Should I turn on Do Not Disturb mode for you?”

If Siri gets to know your habits, it could start making smart suggestions without you even asking.

Will Apple Intelligence Work on All iPhones?

This is the big question: will older iPhones support this next-gen Siri update?

Rumours suggest that Apple Intelligence may require more advanced hardware, meaning only the latest iPhones will fully benefit from the AI-powered Siri. If you have an iPhone 15 Pro or newer, you’ll likely get the full experience.

However, Apple traditionally supports older devices for many years, so while older iPhones might not get the most powerful AI features, they could still see improvements in voice recognition and response times.

What About Privacy?

Apple has built its brand on privacy, and with AI handling personal data, many users will wonder: will Siri’s new AI features be safe?

  • On-device processing: Apple aims to process as much data as possible directly on your iPhone rather than relying on cloud servers.
  • Minimal data collection: Unlike other AI assistants that rely heavily on gathering user data, Apple remains committed to limiting data access.
  • End-to-end encryption: Your Siri requests will remain private and protected from unauthorized access.

Apple’s focus on security means we’re likely to get a smarter Siri without compromising our personal information.

Final Thoughts: Is This the Siri Upgrade We’ve Been Waiting For?

For years, Siri has lagged behind its competitors, but the introduction of Apple Intelligence in iOS 18.5 could finally change that.

With smarter responses, better personalization, and natural conversations, Siri might finally become the AI assistant Apple users deserve. However, whether Apple truly delivers on its promises remains to be seen.

For now, the picture is still drawn from rumours, and Apple has a long history of promising more than it ships on day one. If even part of this comes to pass, it could quietly reshape how we use our iPhones every day.
