# Namaz

*Prayer Times & Qibla*

Namaz is a quiet companion for the five daily prayers. It shows the next prayer and a soft countdown over a living sky that moves with the day, points you toward the Qibla, and reminds you a little before each prayer. Everything is worked out on your device — no accounts, no servers, no tracking.

## About accuracy

Prayer times are computed on your device. Namaz is built with Dawoodi Bohra practice in mind: if your community keeps an official timetable, you can calibrate once and everything lines up to the minute. The familiar calculation methods (MWL, ISNA, Umm al-Qura, Egyptian, Karachi, Tehran) are available too.
