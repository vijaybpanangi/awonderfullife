# Namaz — Privacy Policy

*Namaz — Prayer Times & Qibla · Last updated 21 July 2026*

**The short version: Namaz collects nothing about you.** There are no accounts, no servers of ours, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads. The app calculates everything on your device and works in Airplane Mode.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** We do not operate any servers, and we never receive, see, or store any information about you or your device. There is no account to create and nothing to sign in to.

## Your location

If you allow it, Namaz uses your device's location **only on your device** to calculate the correct prayer times and the direction of the Qibla for where you are. Your location is never transmitted to us or stored anywhere we control. You can also set a location manually, or deny location access entirely and enter it yourself.

## Weather (optional)

Namaz has an optional **Dynamic Weather** feature that tints the sky to match current conditions. When it is turned on, your device requests weather from **Apple's WeatherKit** service, which receives an approximate location in order to return the forecast. This exchange is between your device and Apple and is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/); we never receive or store it. You can turn Dynamic Weather off at any time in Settings, and the app works fully without it.

## Notifications

Prayer reminders are scheduled as **local notifications** on your device. They are not sent through any push server — there is no server involved at all.

## Data stored on your device

Your settings and preferences (such as reminder times, calculation method, and calibration) are stored locally on your device. They stay on your device and are removed when you delete the app.

## Third parties

Namaz includes no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only external service it can contact is Apple's WeatherKit, and only when you enable Dynamic Weather, as described above. Standard Apple system services (such as the notification and location frameworks) operate under Apple's own policies.

## Children's privacy

Namaz is safe for users of all ages. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it collects no data from children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Because the app collects nothing, we do not expect meaningful changes.
